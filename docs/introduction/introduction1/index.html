Background

KYC is know your customer, are the requirements from regulatory for a public services company such as financial institution to know and understand about their customer.

One of the idea to knowing the customer is to eliminate the bank become a platform to do crime such as

  • Money Laundry
  • Terrorism
  • High Risk People
  • Politically Expose Person
  • Sanction List

In most cases KYC need customer to visit branch or at least meet the authorize person in financial institution to identify them and get to know them as part of compliance.

In the new digital world, people can open bank account anywhere anytime, so this become new challenge for financial service provider and also regulation to tap this issue. Therefore, there’s new trend on requirement for e-KYC solutions, where customer can identify them self without required to meet the person directly
